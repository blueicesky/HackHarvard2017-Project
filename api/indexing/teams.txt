<b>Notes:</b><br>
<strong>TSR</strong> – Total Shots Ratio/Percentage <br>
A ratio to explain how teams fare against their average competition in the shots battle. Ex: If Manchester City has 20 shots in the match and Newcastle have 10, City’s TSR for that match is 67%, Newcastle’s is 33%.<br>
<strong>SoTR</strong> – Shots on Target Ratio/Percentage<br>
<strong>Shoot %</strong> - Percentage of total shots on target that have scored.<br>
<strong>Save %</strong> - Percentage of opposition total shots on target that have been saved.<br>
<strong>PDO</strong> - the sum of a teams shooting percentage (goals/shots on target) and its save percentage (saves/shots on target against).
